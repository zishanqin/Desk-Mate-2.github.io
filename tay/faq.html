FAQ

1. What kinds of room I can join?

Answer:

We have different kinds of rooms in different buildings.

Teaching Building

  - Silent room. Only host can public news and messages.

Computer Lab

 - Public chat room. Every participant can talk in the chatbox and is open to everyone satisfying the room restrictions.

Activity Center

 - Public vents will be held occasionally here.

Self-Study Lab

 - Private chat room. Can only enter by invitation and by typing room number.


2. How can I join the room?

Answer:

Click on “Join a room” button in the top menu and you will see a map, then click on the corresponding building that you want to join. Select the room you want join or type the room number to join.


3. How to create a room?

Answer:

Click on “Create a room” button in the top menu and fill in the form to finish the setting of the room. You will get a room number. Then feel free to invite your friends to join!
