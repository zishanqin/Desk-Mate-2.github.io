What we do

We are a Hackie team that strives to help improve the social life of people who work from home during quarantine, helping them regain their usual lifestyle and interact socially with their peers in between tiresome study/work sessions.

Feeling lonely working from home? Bored without colleagues and friends around to work with? Miss the days of studying in the library?

Why not join us and recapture your old life virtually?

Table Mate creates a virtual atmosphere with libraries, study rooms, workspaces and discussion forums, you can always find a space that suits you and share it with your friends.
